About

What is challah?

Challah is a bread traditionally eaten by Jews on Friday to celebrate the coming of Shabbat, the day of rest. That doesn't mean it's only for Jewish people, though! Challah is delicious and everyone can enjoy it.

What is Challah Club?

Challah Club is a service that simplifies ordering challah (from Irving's Premium Challah) each week. Your order will be delivered to Kehillah on Friday during school, and all orders through Challah Club receive a 40% discount!

FAQs

What kinds of challah do you have?

We offer plain, sesame, and poppy seed challahs.

How do I pay?

You can choose to pay either with Venmo or in person. If you choose Venmo, please make it out to @abby-domine.

If you choose in person, just bring money to school.

How do you order for the semester?

If you don't want to keep ordering each week, order for the semester instead! If you're interested, please send me an email at bdomine2024@kehillahstudent.org.

Can I order challah for the staff?

Sure! Just check the box when ordering, and put any special instructions in the comments box.

What's the price of one challah?

Each challah is $6.75.

Anything else? Leave any special instructions in the comments box, and send me an email (bdomine2024@kehillahstudent.org) if you have any questions!
